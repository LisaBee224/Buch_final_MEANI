Blueberry Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 cup blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt (low-fat vanilla or 0% greek yogurt)
  • 3-4 ice cubes
  • splash of apple juice (optional)

  • Put all ingredients in a blender. Blend away on high until smooth. Enjoy!

Green Monster Smoothie

Ingredients

  • 2 cups spinach
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 tbs lemon juice
  • 2 stalks celery
  • 1/2 cucumber
  • 1/2 cup of apple juice

  • Put all ingredients in a blender. Blend away on high until all is blended. Get your green on!
